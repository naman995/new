Custom Crating

DGM California provides custom crating services to protect valuable, dangerous or delicate items during shipping or transportation. We design our crates to meet the specific needs of items that may require extra protection due to their size, shape, fragility, or value. The custom crates are made from sturdy materials such as ISPM-15 certified Heat Treated wood, plywood and can include various moisture proofing, vacuum sealing options depending on the item's requirements and the shipping method

This ensures that the contents remain intact and undamaged throughout the shipping process, including protection from impacts, vibrations, and weather conditions. The customization options include crates tailored to fit unique shapes or sizes precisely, additional features like cushioning, bracing, or moisture protection, marking, handling and labeling with stencils, and on-site service.

Custom crating services

Custom crating services are commonly used in industries such as art and antiques transportation, electronics, medical equipment, industrial machinery, and any other field where standard packaging may not suffice. Our expertise with Dangerous Goods ensures that not only is the cargo packed properly for safe transportation during international and domestic transit but is also compliant with any Overpack requirements for Dangerous Goods packaging.

  • On-site services
  • Protection including Moisture Barriers, Poly Bags, Vacuum
  • Packaging including Vermiculite, Packaging Peanuts, Foam
  • ISPM-15 Heat Treated Wood
  • Stenciling
  • Load Blocking, Bracing of Trailers and Ocean containers
