Custom Projects

DGM California takes up custom engineering, fabrication, assembly projects on-demand basis for unique requirements.Our engineering leadership and staff can provide the necessary expertise and collaboration for any technical projects.

DGM California has provided custom wheels fabrication for ground use of heavy machinery using retreaded aircraft tires. Flaps, Tubes and tires from non-flyable aircraft tires can provide an technologically advanced and yet economical way to cater to tough ground use. We have also helped plan the supply chain and logistics for carbon cleaning machines used in diesel engines and generators servicing. These advanced machinery are used to improve the efficiency and remove carbon deposits to increase the life and serviceability of large diesel engines.

Aircraft retreaded tires for ground use

We can provide retreaded aircraft tires for ground use in agricultural, mining and industrial applications. Aviation tires are high quality tires, are puncture resistant, have high load capacities, and are available in several tread patterns. We can customize tires to the needs and specifications of a variety of applications including for usage on heavy equipment, mining, boat bumpers, agricultural use.

Custom wheels fabrication

We make custom wheels and hubs for use on industrial, agricultural machinery. Paired with non-flyable ground use aircraft tires, the wheels provide an economic option to new hubs and wheels and new tires.
