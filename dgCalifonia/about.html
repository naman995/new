Who We Are

About Us

We have been a pioneer in the Dangerous Goods compliance and transportation industry for over 18 years in the San Francisco Bay Area
and delivering valuable expertise, moving freight and solving complex regulatory compliance issues for all our customers.

Simplifying Complex Regulations

Regulations for transport of dangerous goods and hazardous materials have become increasingly complex and vary by mode of transport (ground, air and ocean); state and country of operation and the carriers, who have Special Provisions over and above the government regulations. The role of an effective Dangerous Goods Advisor has become increasingly important as a facilitator of global trade and e-commerce and to avoid costly penalties and punishments for non-compliance.

History

Lack of reputable providers with science background

DGM California was founded in 2006 by two professionals who could not find any service provider to prepare the documentation to export chemicals, and saw an opportunity to provide a standardized, easily accessible and better level of service to a thriving startup ecosystem. Since our founding, the science and engineering background of our founders has allowed us to become an authoritative source of expertise in regulated products transportation.
Remember, there are about 3600 UN numbers in the dangerous goods framework. Would you trust your cargo to someone without an advanced Chemistry background?
